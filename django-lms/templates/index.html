Got questions?

Check out our FAQ section to learn more about Handyskills

What is Handyskills?

Handyskills is the first online learning management system (LMS) that offers courses and training programs about handskills in order to boost the development of Africa. It is designed to provide users with a personalized and engaging learning experience.

Who can use Handyskills?

Handyskills is open to anyone who wants to learn and improve their skills. Our courses are designed for individuals, businesses, and organizations looking to develop their knowledge and skills.

How do I sign up for Handyskills?

You can sign up for Handyskills by visiting our website and clicking on the "Sign Up" button. You will need to provide some basic information, such as your name and email address, to create an account.

How much do Handyskills courses cost?

The cost of courses on Handyskills varies depending on the subject, the level of the course, and the instructor. Some courses are free, while others may cost a couple of dollars. You can view the price of a course on the course page before enrolling.

What is the refund policy for Handyskills courses?

The refund policy for Handyskills courses varies depending on the course and the instructor. Some courses may offer a full refund within a certain timeframe, while others may not offer refunds at all. You can view the refund policy for a course on the course page before enrolling.
